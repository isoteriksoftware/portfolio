AN AUTOMATIC OVERHEAD TANK WATER LEVEL MONITORING AND CONTROL SYSTEM



In Nigeria, most domestic water supply from the water authority are channeled into an underground reservoir from where it is pump into the overhead tank. A major challenge remains how to monitor the water level in the overhead tank to know when to power on and off the pump. In this regard, this project developed an automatic water level monitoring and controlled system using an ultrasonic sensor to monitor the water level
