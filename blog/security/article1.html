DEVELOPMENT AND IMPLEMENTATION OF A SMART DOORLOCK USING FINGERPRINT INTERFACE



The project aim at designing and implementing an effective fingerprint door lock system. This system helps in solving the problem of security in preventing unauthorized access into the home or office . To solve the problem of security challenges at home/offices, a biometric verification method was adopted using the fingerprint , the biometric verification gives a unique identification to the authorized personnel as it is impossible for two people to have identical fingerprint.
