Development of An IoT Smart Electrical Appliances Power Consumption Monitoring and Control Device



An IoT smart electrical appliances power consumption monitoring and control device was developed. This entailed the utilization of an ACS712 current sensor, ESP8266 microcontroller, digital display, relay, and buzzer module for the development of the proposed device. The current sensor was interfaced with the microcontroller to measure the energy consumption rate of each appliance and output such information on the digital display connected to the microcontroller.
