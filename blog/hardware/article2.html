A SMART BABY WALKING ASSISTIVE SYSTEM (BABY WALKER)



Motherhood comes with a lot of joy from birth to gradual developmental process of the baby. This include the crawling stage and the process of learning the act of walking, which is the most challenging stage in the child developmental process. As such several babies walking assistive device have been developed. However, a major challenge remains the need for the manual stimulation of the device under the supervision of the care giver. Therefore this project addresses this challenge by developing a smart baby walking assistive system that uses ultrasonic sensor to sense the presence of an obstacle once a the system is being triggered via the control signal sent to the Arduino uno for the movement of the wheel.
